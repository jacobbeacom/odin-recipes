Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for some delicious homemade mac and cheese.

This is a nice and rich mac and cheese, serve with a salad for a quick and easy meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp chedder cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmeesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika
